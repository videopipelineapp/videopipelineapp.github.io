Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026 · Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Video Pipeline ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our automated video publishing tool. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. About the App

Video Pipeline is an automated video publishing tool that uses third-party platform APIs — including TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram — to upload pre-produced video content to accounts that you own and have explicitly connected.

2. Information We Access

  • Account authorization tokens. When you connect a platform account, we receive access tokens issued by that platform so the App can publish on your behalf.
  • Basic account information. Such as the account/channel identifier and display name needed to target the correct destination.
  • Content you provide. The video files, titles, descriptions, and metadata you choose to publish.

We do not collect your platform passwords. Authorization is handled through each platform's official OAuth flow.

3. How We Use Information

  • To publish your videos to the platforms and accounts you select.
  • To track the status of each upload and report success or failure to you.
  • To refresh authorization tokens so connected accounts continue to work.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Authorization tokens and operational data are stored only as long as needed to operate the App for you. You can revoke the App's access at any time from the connected platform's settings, after which the corresponding tokens are no longer usable.

5. Third-Party Platforms

The App interacts with TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram through their official APIs. Your use of those platforms is governed by their own terms and privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect access tokens and operational data from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
